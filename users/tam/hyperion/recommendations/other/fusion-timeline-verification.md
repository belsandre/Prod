## Why This Matters

### 1. Thesis-Driven vs Retroactive Framing

**Thesis-Driven (Strong)**:
- Develop conviction → publish analysis → make investments
- Shows foresight and independent thinking
- Demonstrates ability to identify emerging sectors early

**Retroactive Framing (Concerning)**:
- Make investments → construct narrative around them
- Shows marketing skill, not foresight
- Questions authenticity of thought leadership

### 2. Pattern with Other Claims

**Similar Timing Questions**:
- Quantinuum investment (March 2020) before Substack launch (~2021)
- First article (October 2021) after multiple investments
- "Since 2019" claim with zero 2019 timeline events

### 3. Thought Leadership Value

**If Article Preceded Investments**:
- Validates analytical capability
- Shows prescience in sector
- Strengthens deal flow claims ("CEOs cite article")

**If Investments Preceded Article**:
- Questions article purpose (marketing vs research)
- Weakens "early positioning" claim
- May indicate pattern of narrative construction
