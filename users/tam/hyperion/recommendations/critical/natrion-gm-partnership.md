**Priority**: CRITICAL (Dataroom Credibility Issue)

**Owner**: Investment Team

## Executive Summary

The dataroom claims Natrion has a "collaboration with GM" implying a customer relationship, but research found **GM is actually an investor (GM Ventures)**, not a customer. This misrepresentation raises serious questions about dataroom credibility and whether other portfolio company claims are similarly misleading.

**Recommendation**: Request documentation of the actual Natrion-GM relationship and clarify why dataroom presented investor relationship as customer "collaboration."

---

## Why This Is Critical

### 1. Customer vs Investor Is Material Distinction

**What Dataroom Implies**: GM is a customer/partner with commercial deployment

**What Research Found**: GM Ventures is an investor in Natrion

**Why This Matters**:
- Customer relationship = product-market fit validation
- Investor relationship = financial interest only
- Conflating these misrepresents company traction
