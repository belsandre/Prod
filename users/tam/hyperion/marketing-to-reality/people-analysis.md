**Entity:** Hyperion Investment Management, LLC
**General Partners:** Dillon Dunteman (Founder & GP), Henry Bellew (Co-GP)
**Analysis Date:** 2025-11-19

**Analysis Goal:** Validate GP credentials, networks, personal attribution, and team structure against independent evidence.

**Sources:** Dataroom (12 files: GP Bio, pitch deck, case studies, testimonials), research/people (LinkedIn profiles, network CSVs with ~1,700 connections, Harvard/Vista/Firmament/Raine verification), claims files (claims-analysis.md, claims.json), independent sources (Harvard records, news, transaction announcements)

**Process:** Extracted GP claims ‚Üí mapped evidence by tier (Tier 3 independent, Tier 2 affiliated, Tier 1 dataroom) ‚Üí validated credentials/networks/attribution ‚Üí identified 5 critical people gaps ‚Üí synthesized team assessment

**Note:** People-level analysis only. Portfolio performance verification addressed in separate portfolio analysis workflows.

## Executive Summary

Hyperion Ventures' GP team has **largely credible credentials** with verified Harvard educations, confirmed employment at respected institutions (Vista Equity Partners, Firmament, The Raine Group), and legitimate backgrounds. However, **significant attribution ambiguities and structural team concerns** warrant careful scrutiny.

**Key People-Specific Findings:**

- **Dillon Dunteman (Founder & GP):** Strong intellectual preparation with 4+ years of public deeptech thought leadership (Substack since 2021), verified Harvard engineering credentials, confirmed Vista/Firmament employment, and ~1,700 LinkedIn connections across relevant networks. However, **four people-level concerns** remain: (1) **Vista role ambiguity**‚Äîunclear whether he sourced/led deals or participated passively, (2) **Firmament attribution**‚Äîboth exits occurred 14-22 months after departure, (3) **Timeline overlap capacity**‚Äî24 angel investments while full-time at Vista raises bandwidth questions, (4) **"Early positioning since 2019"** lacks archived pre-2020 public evidence.

- **Henry Bellew (Co-GP):** Excellent Harvard academic credentials (A.B. 2023, cum laude) with impressive athletic achievements, but **very limited professional experience** (~2 years post-graduation) and **virtually absent from dataroom** (1/30 pages, zero case studies, zero testimonials) raising fundamental questions about true partnership vs. junior support role.

**Structural Team Concerns:** This is effectively a **single-GP fund** with Dillon handling all critical functions (investment strategy, sourcing, diligence, portfolio support) while Henry's role remains completely undefined. This creates **single point of failure risk** and raises capacity questions about one GP supporting 20-30 portfolio companies.

**Overall Assessment:** Team credentials are **largely credible but attribution of personal contributions is highly ambiguous**. Success depends heavily on Dillon's individual capability‚Äîa risk amplified by undefined Co-GP role and lack of team redundancy.
