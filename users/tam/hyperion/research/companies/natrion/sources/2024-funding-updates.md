### Critical Discrepancy vs. Dataroom

**Dataroom Claims** (September 15, 2022 entry):
- Entry valuation: $12M post-money
- Expected raise: $60M-$100M minimum in Q1 '24
- **"GM signing $60m-$100m commitment"**

**Public Reality** (2024):
- December 2024: $4.4M convertible note (NOT $60M-$100M)
- **No evidence of GM partnership, investment, or commitment**
- Total funding only $11.3M (across all rounds since 2018)
- Company still fundraising (not completed major round)

**Discrepancy Analysis**:
- **87% shortfall**: $4.4M vs. $60M-$100M projected
- **GM commitment**: No public evidence (major red flag)
- **Timeline miss**: Q1 '24 target, actual Dec '24 much smaller raise
- **Possible explanations**:
  1. GM deal fell through
  2. GM discussions never materialized
  3. Dataroom information was speculative/aspirational
  4. Deal is private/not yet announced (unlikely for $60M-$100M)
  5. Market conditions changed (EV/battery market softness)

**Market Context**:
EV market has slowed 2024-2025, which may explain funding challenges. Many battery startups struggling to raise at expected valuations. GM has been more cautious with EV investments recently.
