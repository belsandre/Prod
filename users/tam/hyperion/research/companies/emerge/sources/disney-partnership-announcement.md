## Raw Content Summary

**Announcement - June 1, 2023**

Emerge, Los Angeles-based multi-sensory communication platform company, announced strategic multi-year partnership with The Walt Disney Company. Partnership will integrate beloved Disney content into Emerge's personal connection platform.

**Partnership Details**:
Starting with one of Disney's top franchises, fans will be able to share multi-sensory experiences and interact with friends and family across the world from home. Users can physically feel "virtual high five" or experience favorite superpowers with bare hands, no gloves or controllers needed.

**Technology**:
Emerge Wave-1 enables hand-to-hand tactile contact through mid-air ultrasonic force fields. Platform combines sight, touch, sound, and brain activity, powered by emotion AI. Supports Smart TVs, VR, and AR headsets.

**Disney Quote**:
Eric Bautista, Disney's Director of Consumer Products, Games, and Publishing: "Thrilled to leverage their technology platform to add a deeper level of 'feeling' to these stories."

**Strategic Vision**:
Partnership makes sensory experiences more tangible, extending Disney's storytelling capabilities into haptic/tactile domain. Multi-year engagement indicates long-term strategic commitment from both parties.

**Implementation**:
Specific franchise, timeline, pricing, and availability not disclosed in announcement. Partnership focused on enhancing fan engagement and creating new ways to experience Disney IP.
