# Normal Computing: Company Overview

## Mission & Vision
Normal Computing is rethinking semiconductors from the ground up. The company applies AI to accelerate complex hardware engineering with zero defects through a unified EDA (Electronic Design Automation) platform.

## Core Products

**Normal EDA**
- A unified, secure, collaborative workspace for creating verification collateral
- Powered by an "autoformalizing" AI model series
- Enables 2x faster time-to-market for semiconductor partners

**Normal ASICs**
- Thermodynamic computing hardware designed to reduce AI energy consumption by potentially 1000x
- Co-designed with Normal EDA for diffusion and simulation workloads
- Operates near physical efficiency limits

## Technology Approach
The company deploys AI software to solve semiconductor engineering challenges. Their thermodynamic computing approach represents a physics-based redesign of how computing hardware handles AI workloads, addressing energy consumption as a critical constraint.

## Market Position
Leading semiconductor companies (Fortune 500 level) use Normal EDA to scale hardware development. The platform targets the semiconductor industry's most pressing challenges in verification and design efficiency.

## Recent Developments
- Announced tape-out of the world's first thermodynamic computing chip (August 2025)
- Backs Maryland Quantum-Thermodynamics Hub research initiatives
- Active publishing on semiconductor engineering topics in industry publications
