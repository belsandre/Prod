## Key Information

### Company Mission
"Efficient Batteries for a Renewable Future" - Developing efficient battery materials for renewable energy applications through scalable, manufacturability-focused innovations.

### Core Technology: Gen-1 Active Separator

**Base Technology**: LISIC (lithium ceramic-polymer composite) electrolyte

**Performance Specifications**:
- **50% improvement in specific energy density** vs. legacy batteries
- **Operating temperature**: -50°C to 250°C (vs. -15°C to 60°C for state-of-the-art Li-ion)
- **Fast charging**: 20-30 minutes
- **Thermal stability**: Benign failure response (no thermal runaway)
- **Manufacturing compatibility**: Direct replacement in existing gigascale production lines (no retooling required)

### Product Suite

**X-12™ Li-Metal Anode**:
- Lithium metal anode technology
- Modular component

**M3™ Wetting Agent**:
- Electrolyte wetting enhancement
- Improves performance

**Customizable Cathodes**:
- Compatible with NMC, LFP, LNMO, and other cathode chemistries
- Flexibility for different applications

### Key Differentiator
**"Manufacturer-Minded" Innovation**:
- Designs solutions that work within existing manufacturing infrastructure
- No factory retooling required
- Cost parity with conventional materials
- Removes economic barriers to adoption
