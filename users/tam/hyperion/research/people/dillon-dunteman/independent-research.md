## Executive Summary

This research focused exclusively on verifying the backgrounds, credentials, and claims of Hyperion Investment Management's two general partners: Dillon Dunteman (Founder & General Partner) and Henry Bellew (General Partner). The research found that **most core claims about education, employment history, and achievements are verifiable**, though important nuances and some inconsistencies emerged.

**Dillon Dunteman** presents a credible profile with verified Harvard engineering credentials (S.B. '19), confirmed employment at both Firmament (2019-2021) and Vista Equity Partners (2021-present), and demonstrated thought leadership through his "Prescience" Substack (explicitly labeled "Precursor to Hyperion"). However, **key attribution questions remain** about his personal role in Firmament's exits (which occurred after his departure) and his specific contributions to Vista's $1B+ in deployments. His personal investment track record (6.7x TVPI / 77% IRR) could not be independently verified but appears plausible given timing and market conditions.

**Henry Bellew** has verified Harvard credentials (A.B. 2023, cum laude) and impressive rowing achievements, including confirmed championship wins. However, **two inconsistencies warrant clarification**: (1) his claimed Henley Royal Regatta "semi-finalist" status appears to reference a 2024 achievement that occurred after his 2023 graduation, and (2) his connection to The Raine Group could not be verified on their current team page, though "advisor" language in the pitch deck may be intentionally softer than "employee."

Overall assessment: **The team's credentials are largely legitimate, but attribution of specific deals/achievements should be carefully examined, and identified inconsistencies should be clarified during due diligence.**
