## Raw Content Summary

**Press Release - October 5, 2023**

Emerge, a Los Angeles-based technology company, announced partnership with Sony Electronics to integrate its multi-sensory communication platform into Sony SmartTV product lines, launching mid-2024 on Android TV-compatible devices.

**Product**:
Emerge Wave-1 emits sculpted ultrasonic waves allowing users to feel and interact via touch in virtual environments with bare hands. Device integrates touch, sight, sound, and brain activity for multi-sensory experiences. Platform includes Emotion AI to digitize and transmit emotions in real-time.

**Deployment**:
Sony will bundle Emerge Wave-1 with Bravia Cam, enabling gesture control and tactile feedback for video streaming, cloud gaming, and video calls. Launch targeted for mid-2024 on compatible Sony Android TV devices.

**Market**:
Smart TV adoption reaches 87% of US population with 1.3 billion internet-enabled TV devices globally, providing massive distribution opportunity.

**Strategic Vision**:
Emerge co-CEOs: "Partnership enables virtual high fives and holding hands across globe, addressing loneliness epidemic."
Sony VP Nick Colsey: "Partnership explores how tactile video can help families feel closer across distance."

**Availability**:
Wave-1 currently available in limited beta via early access program, supporting Meta Quest 2. Expanding to Smart TVs and spatial computing platforms.
