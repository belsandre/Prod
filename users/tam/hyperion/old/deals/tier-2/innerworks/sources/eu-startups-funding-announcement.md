## Raw Content

**Headline**: London-based cybersecurity startup Innerworks raises €3.7 million to stop AI fraud in its tracks

**Published**: August 18, 2025

**Summary**:
Innerworks, a London-based cybersecurity startup specializing in detecting and preventing AI-driven fraud through its proprietary Synthetic Threat Intelligence technology, announced a €3.7 million ($4 million) seed funding round in August 2025.

**Company Background**:
Founded in 2020 by Oliver Quie, Oscar Hayek, Tom Ryan, and Cat Mcdonald, Innerworks has built a security platform capable of identifying synthetic, AI-generated interactions with 97% accuracy. The system has already been tested in national infrastructure defence, collaborations with major digital asset platforms, and investigations into some of the largest cryptocurrency breaches on record.

**Investors**:
The funding round was led by AlbionVC and included participation from Crypto.com, Digital Currency Group, Firestreak Ventures, Founders Capital, Metaversal Ventures, NVTBL Ventures, and RedTeam.

**Technology**:
Innerworks' core innovation centers on "Synthetic Threat Intelligence," designed to stop AI fraud by identifying and neutralizing artificial intelligence-based fraudulent activities before they cause damage. The company's platform distinguishes authentic human behavior from AI-driven fraud attempts.

Central to Innerworks' offering is RedTeam, a crypto-bounty programme that enlists ethical hackers and AI agents from around the globe to stress-test the system and expose vulnerabilities.

**Market Context**:
The funding comes as AI fraud losses have surpassed €1.3 billion in Europe, with sophisticated AI technologies increasingly weaponized for fraud. This makes Innerworks' detection capabilities particularly relevant for financial services and security-focused enterprises.

**Use of Funds**:
The seed funding will support the expansion of Innerworks' platform capabilities, particularly around adversarial AI training, and accelerate its global reach. The company also aims to establish new partnerships with top-tier financial institutions working to counteract rising synthetic threats.

**Performance**:
Innerworks has achieved a 97% detection rate against even the most sophisticated AI threats, outperforming the accuracy of multibillion-dollar companies and all top five incumbent providers in the space.

**CEO Statement**:
Oliver Quie and team emphasized their mission to build "the Internet's immune system" to protect against AI-generated fraud and synthetic threats.
