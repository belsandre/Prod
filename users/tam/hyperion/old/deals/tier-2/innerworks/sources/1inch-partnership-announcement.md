1inch has integrated Innerworks' Synthetic Threat Intelligence technology to enhance its security infrastructure. The implementation includes:
- Device intelligence for detecting AI-generated fraud
- RedTeam ethical hacking platform for continuous security testing
- Real-time threat monitoring and prevention

DeFi security has become increasingly critical as AI-powered fraud attempts have grown more sophisticated. The partnership addresses the need for advanced detection capabilities in the decentralized finance ecosystem.

**Technology Implementation**:
Innerworks' platform provides 1inch with the ability to distinguish between authentic human behavior and AI-driven fraud attempts with 97% accuracy. The RedTeam component enables continuous security testing through ethical hackers and AI agents.

**Significance**:
The partnership validates Innerworks' technology for production deployment in high-stakes financial environments. 1inch's adoption demonstrates the platform's readiness for enterprise-scale DeFi security applications.

**Timing**:
This partnership was announced in October 2025, just two months after Innerworks' €3.7M seed funding round, indicating rapid commercial traction and sales execution.
