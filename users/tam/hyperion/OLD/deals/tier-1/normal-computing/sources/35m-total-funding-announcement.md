# Normal Computing Corp: Funding, Mission & Technology

## Funding
**Total Raised:** $35M in seed capital

**Investors:** Eric Schmidt (FSV), Celesta Capital, Drive Capital, UK Advanced Research + Innovation Agency, Micron Ventures, Samsung Next, Intel Ignite, and National Security Innovation Network

## Company Mission
Normal Computing aims to "rethink how we predictably uncover drastically better chips" by building "mathematical and physical intelligence to accelerate hardware innovation." The company addresses the challenge that "complexity of chips is exploding" alongside design and testing timelines, with an emphasis on achieving silicon supremacy before hitting Moore's Wall.

## Technology Roadmap
The company uses a three-phase approach called "Ctrl+R: refreshing hardware":

- **2025:** AI design and verification of hardware logic from specifications
- **2027:** Thermodynamic computing integration for custom silicon at energy efficiency limits
- **2028:** Safe recursive self-improvement where AI revises its own design specifications

## Founding & Team
Founded in 2022 by engineers from Google Brain and Google X, the leadership includes Faris Sbahi, Antonio Martinez, and Yui-Hong "Matthias" Tan. The team operates from offices in New York City, San Francisco, London, and Copenhagen.

## Customers & Partnerships
"Fortune 500 companies use our AI systems to scale new hardware and enable faster time-to-market," though specific client names are not disclosed in available materials.
