### Product Portfolio

**Helios System** (Latest, Launched November 2025):
- 98 physical qubits (barium ions)
- 99.921% two-qubit gate fidelity
- 99.9975% one-qubit gate fidelity
- 48 error-corrected logical qubits
- QCCD (Quantum Charged Coupled Device) architecture
- NVQLink integration with NVIDIA GB200 processors

**H-Series Systems** (Legacy):
- Predecessor systems using ytterbium qubits
- Up to 56 qubits
- Cloud-accessible via quantum computing service

**Software Stack**:
- **Guppy**: Modern Python-based programming language for quantum computing
- **Quantum Operating System**: Management and orchestration of quantum hardware
- **Error Correction**: Real-time error correction capabilities
- **Hybrid Computing**: Seamless integration of quantum and classical computing

**Quantum Cybersecurity**:
- Quantum-safe cryptography solutions
- Quantum key distribution
- Post-quantum cryptography preparation

### Technology Approach

**Trapped-Ion Quantum Computing**:
- **Method**: Individual charged atoms (ions) trapped and manipulated using electromagnetic fields and lasers
- **Advantages**:
  - High gate fidelity (99.921% demonstrated)
  - Long coherence times
  - All-to-all qubit connectivity
  - Individual qubit addressability
- **Architecture**: QCCD allows efficient qubit routing and scaling

**Full-Stack Integration**:

- Quantum algorithms and software development
- Cloud infrastructure and services
- Professional services and support

**Differentiators** (per company website):
