## Executive Summary

Innerworks is a London-based cybersecurity startup founded in 2020 that specializes in detecting AI-generated fraud and synthetic threats. The company raised €3.7M ($4M) seed funding in **August 2025** led by AlbionVC, slightly later than the dataroom's Q1 '24 projection but at presumably higher valuation.

**Technology**: "Synthetic Threat Intelligence" platform with 97% accuracy in detecting AI-generated fraud, deepfakes, and synthetic interactions. Focus on financial services, crypto, and infrastructure protection.

**Key Validation**: Partnership with 1inch (leading DeFi platform), national infrastructure defense testing, involvement in major cryptocurrency breach investigations.

**Dataroom Alignment**: ✅ Seed round materialized as expected (August 2025 vs. Q1 '24 projection), validating investment thesis.
