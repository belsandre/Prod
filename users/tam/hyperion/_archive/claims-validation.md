## Executive Summary

This report validates marketing claims made by Hyperion Ventures GP Dillon Dunteman by cross-referencing 6 KEY claims and 24 sub-claims against an objective timeline constructed from independent research. The analysis reveals:

**Key Findings**:
- ‚úÖ **Verified**: 8 claims (33%)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Partially Verified**: 9 claims (38%)
- ‚ùì **Unverified**: 5 claims (21%)
- ‚ùå **Conflicting Evidence**: 1 claim (4%)
- üïê **Timing Issues**: 1 claim (4%)

**Critical Issues**:
1. **Funding Discrepancies**: Multiple portfolio companies show significant gaps between dataroom funding amounts and public announcements (Dirac: $6M vs $10.7M, Normal: $17M vs $35M)
2. **Unverifiable Value-Add**: Most value-add claims (hiring referrals, VC introductions, advisory roles) have NO independent verification‚Äîall from Tier 1 (dataroom) sources
3. **Customer Claims Contradicted**: Dirac claims "pilots with Anduril, Stellantis, Lockheed Martin" but NO public evidence found despite comprehensive research
4. **Missing Investment Details**: 18 of 24 portfolio companies lack timeline events; fusion investments (Zap, Avalanche, Hephaestus, Marathon) have no dates
5. **Failed Investment Opacity**: 3 underperforming investments acknowledged but NOT named or dated in dataroom

**Retroactive Framing Risk**: Thought leadership (Substack) launched ~2021 AFTER first investment (Quantinuum March 2020), suggesting narrative may be retrospectively constructed around early wins.
