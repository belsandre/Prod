## Recent Milestones & Announcements

**2025 Product Roadmap:**
- Omni-channel automation expanding beyond email-only campaigns
- Person-level lookalike matching using LinkedIn profiles to identify similar prospects
- Shift to consumption-based pricing model with platform access fee

**User Traction:**
Founder reported generating a 37.5% reply rate on web3 accounting sector outbound campaigns using only targeting and messaging, achieved without AI personalization or behavioral signals.

**Customer Validation:**
The company emphasized that referrals have become their largest lead source, with customers organically promoting Scout without incentives.

**Holiday 2024 Initiative:**
Offered free campaign setup with a guarantee of 5+ qualified leads within one week for eligible founders and sales leaders targeting SMBs.
