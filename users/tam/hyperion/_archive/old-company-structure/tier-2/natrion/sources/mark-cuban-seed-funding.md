## Raw Content

**Press Release - May 25, 2022**

**NEW YORK, May 25, 2022** -- Natrion, Inc., a leader in solid-state battery component manufacturing, today announced it has raised $2 million in an oversubscribed seed funding round led by Chicago-based TechNexus Venture Collaborative. The round included participation from Tamarack Global, Mark Cuban (of Shark Tank fame), Illinois Ventures, and other private investors.

Prior to this private raise, Natrion received a $200,000 Small Business Technology Transfer Research (STTR) contract from the U.S. Naval Air Warfare Center Weapons Division, along with multiple other U.S. Department of Defense contracts.

**About Natrion**:
Founded in 2018 by Alex Kosyakov and Thomas Rouffiac, Natrion develops advanced solid-state battery technology. The company's patented LISIC (Lithium Solid Ionic Composite) polymer-ceramic hybrid solid electrolyte enhances charging speeds, driving range, durability, and safety for electric vehicles while fitting into existing manufacturing processes.

**Mark Cuban Quote**:
"Natrion's streamlined R&D approach and business model are what really differentiate them. The company has major commercial traction in the automotive space and is positioned for significant growth."

**CEO Quote**:
Alex Kosyakov stated: "We have a stellar core research and development team focused on advancing lithium-metal battery technologies. Our investors bring deep industry knowledge and share our commitment to bringing solid-state battery materials to market."

**Use of Funds**:
The seed funding will support Natrion's plans to establish pouch cell prototyping capabilities at their R&D centers in Champaign, IL and Binghamton, NY, with a pilot production line targeted for launch in 2023. The company is in active conversations with transportation sector partners and continues to secure Department of Defense contracts.

**Company Locations**:
- 60 Hazelwood Dr, Champaign, IL
- 120 Hawley St, Binghamton, NY
