### Why Delaware?
**Standard for Venture-Backed Companies**:
- 90%+ of venture-backed startups incorporate in Delaware
- Well-established corporate law and case precedent
- Court of Chancery specialized in business disputes
- Flexible corporate governance structures

- Enables stock option plans, preferred stock, convertible notes, etc.
