## Starlink Laser System Performance Metrics

### Network Scale (January 2024)
- **Fleet Size**: Over 5,000 free-space optical communication terminals deployed
- **Daily Data Throughput**: Over 42 petabytes per day delivered to customers
- **Active Laser Links**: 9,000 lasers operating simultaneously
- **Daily Link Formations**: ~266,141 laser acquisitions per day
- **Data Rate**: Terabits per second aggregate throughput

### Individual Link Performance
- **Link Speed**: 100 Gbps per link baseline
- **Peak Performance**: Up to 200 Gbps achieved
- **Link Uptime**: >99% reliability achieved
- **Link Duration**: Some links sustained for weeks at a time
- **Maximum Distance**: 5,400 kilometers between satellites demonstrated
- **Atmospheric Penetration**: Links cut through atmosphere down to 30 km above Earth's surface

### Manufacturing Capabilities
- **Design Generation**: Gen 3 design (recently upgraded to Gen 4 at time of presentation)
- **Production Rate**: 200 laser communication units per day
- **Manufacturing Approach**: Uses off-the-shelf components for rapid scaling

### Use Cases
- Critical for data relay when ground stations unavailable (over oceans, polar regions)
- Antarctic coverage specifically mentioned
- Enables global mesh network connectivity without ground infrastructure
