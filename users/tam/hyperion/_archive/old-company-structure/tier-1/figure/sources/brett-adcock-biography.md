### Career History

**Vettery (2012-2018)**
- **Founded**: Age 26 in 2012
- **Description**: AI-powered talent marketplace connecting companies with employees
- **Exit**: Acquired in 2018 for $110 million by The Adecco Group (world's largest recruiting firm)
- **Post-acquisition**: Vettery absorbed Hired.com and rebranded under Hired name in 2020
- **Scale**: 300 employees, 20,000 customers, 30,000 interviews per month by 2017

**Archer Aviation (2018-Present)**
- **Founded**: October 2018
- **Description**: Leading aerospace manufacturer focused on electric air taxis
- **Capital Raised**: Over $1 billion
- **Exit**: Went public in 2021 at $2.7 billion valuation (NYSE listing)
- **Achievements**:
  - Personally architected, engineered, and flight-tested five aircraft generations
  - $1.5 billion deal with United Airlines
  - Vertical integration: aircraft design, flight software, actuation systems, electric motors, battery technology
- **Departure**: Left operational role April 2022, remains major shareholder
- **Reason stated**: "Misalignment between me and the board, given we were in the public markets"

**Figure AI (2022-Present)**
- **Founded**: 2022 with $100 million of his own capital (self-financed)
- **Description**: General-purpose humanoid robots addressing labor shortages
- **Current Status**: "End-to-end operations with our first commercial customer, BMW"
- **Applications**: Manufacturing, logistics, warehousing, retail, residential settings
- **Team Source**: Assembled from Boston Dynamics, Tesla, Google DeepMind, Apple

**Cover (2023-Present)**
- **Founded**: 2023 in partnership with NASA's Jet Propulsion Lab
- **Description**: AI security venture creating non-intrusive weapon detection scanners
- **Motivation**: Addressing gun violence
- **Technology**: Detects concealed weapons in crowds without intrusive searches
