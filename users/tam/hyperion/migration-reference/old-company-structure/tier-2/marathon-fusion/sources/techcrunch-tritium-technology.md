## Key Information

### Core Technology: Refined Superpermeation

**Process Overview**:
Marathon has developed a refinement of **superpermeation**, a 40-year-old filtration process using solid metal membranes. The system works by:
1. Converting hydrogen-containing mixtures into plasma
2. Pressing plasma against metal membranes
3. Hydrogen (including tritium) passes through while impurities are blocked
4. Process simultaneously compresses hydrogen to pressure needed for fuel injection systems

**CEO Quote** (Kyle Schiller):
> "The whole idea here is just getting maximal throughput as fast as possible."

**Performance Target**:
Company targets "a few orders of magnitude improvement" in filtration throughput compared to existing experimental equipment.

### The Tritium Supply Problem

**Global Scarcity**:
- Only approximately **20 kilograms of tritium exists globally**
- Commercial fusion plants require several kilograms just for startup
- Current supply derives from nuclear fission plant waste byproducts
- Future fusion plants must breed their own tritium to remain viable (no alternative supply chain)

**Critical Bottleneck**:
Without efficient tritium extraction and recycling, commercial fusion plants cannot operate economically or may not be buildable at all.

### Customer Partnerships

**Letters of Intent Secured**:
1. **Commonwealth Fusion Systems**

   - Approach: Tokamak-based fusion
   - Status: Building SPARC demonstration reactor

2. **Helion Energy**

**Combined Customer Funding**: $2.607 billion validates fusion industry momentum

### Business Model

**Positioning**:
Marathon positions itself as **infrastructure enabler** for fusion commercialization:
- Does not build fusion reactors
- Sells tritium processing systems to fusion companies
- Solves critical bottleneck rather than competing in reactor design
- Can serve multiple fusion customers (not winner-take-all market)

**Value Proposition**:
- Reduces tritium inventory required (lowers operational costs)
- Enables smaller reactor designs (capital cost reduction)
- Makes fusion plants economically viable (fuel self-sufficiency)
