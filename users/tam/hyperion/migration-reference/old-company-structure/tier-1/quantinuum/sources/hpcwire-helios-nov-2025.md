### Technical Coverage

**Helios System Specifications** (per HPCwire article):
- **Physical Qubits**: 98 barium ions
- **Gate Fidelity**: 99.921% (two-qubit), 99.9975% (one-qubit)
- **Logical Qubits**: 48 error-corrected logical qubits with 99.99% fidelity
- **Architecture**: QCCD (Quantum Charged Coupled Device) with ion junction
- **Connectivity**: All-to-all qubit connectivity
- **Performance**: Better-than-physical performance (logical qubits exceed physical qubit performance)

**Technical Analysis** (from article):
- Helios represents significant advancement over H2 predecessor (56 ytterbium qubits → 98 barium qubits)
- Error correction achievement (48 error-corrected logical qubits) positions Quantinuum ahead of competitors
- Trapped-ion approach provides fidelity advantage over superconducting competitors
- QCCD architecture with ion junction enables scalability path
