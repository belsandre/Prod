## Raw Content

SUNNYVALE, Calif., Sept. 16, 2025 /PRNewswire/ -- Figure, a leader in artificial intelligence (AI) and robotics, announced today that it has exceeded more than $1 billion in committed capital through its Series C financing round. The round was led by Parkway Venture Capital with significant investment from Brookfield Asset Management, NVIDIA, Macquarie Capital, Intel Capital, Align Ventures, Tamarack Global, LG Technology Ventures, Salesforce, T-Mobile Ventures, and Qualcomm Ventures, at a post-money valuation of $39 billion.

"This milestone is critical to unlocking the next stage of growth for humanoid robots, scaling out our AI platform Helix and BotQ manufacturing," said Brett Adcock, Founder and CEO of Figure.

The Series C funding will be used to further three areas:

1. Expanding production manufacturing at BotQ and real-world deployments for household and commercial tasks
2. Building next-generation NVIDIA GPU infrastructure to accelerate training and simulation for Helix's core models
3. Launching advanced data collection efforts including human video and multimodal sensory inputs to improve robot perception and operation in dynamic environments

Figure has raised nearly $2 billion since its founding in 2022 and continues to be one of the most sought-after private investments in the AI and robotics space.

About Figure:
Figure is pioneering the development of general-purpose humanoid robots designed to help people live better lives. The company's flagship product, Figure 02, represents the cutting edge of embodied AI, combining advanced hardware and software to perform a wide range of tasks in real-world environments.
