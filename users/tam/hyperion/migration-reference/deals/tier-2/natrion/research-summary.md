## Executive Summary

Natrion is a U.S.-based solid-state battery technology company (founded 2018) developing LISIC (Lithium Solid Ionic Composite) electrolyte technology for EVs and energy storage. The company has raised $11.3M total, including $2M seed (2022, Mark Cuban) and $4.4M convertible note (Dec 2024).

**Key Strengths**:
- Manufacturing-compatible technology (no factory retooling needed)
- Strong government validation (Air Force contracts >$1.5M, DoD support)
- Industry recognition (2024 Edison Award, Forbes 30U30)
- Tier-1 investor (LG Technology Ventures)
- Commercial product launch (taking orders for Gen-1 separator)
- Multiple U.S. facilities (NY, IL)

**Major Concerns**:
- **Critical**: Expected $60M-$100M GM deal did not materialize
- Raised only $4.4M in 2024 vs. $60M-$100M projection
- Total funding ($11.3M) insufficient for battery manufacturing scale
- Still fundraising 2+ years after Hyperion investment
- Highly competitive market (QuantumScape $1.3B raised, Solid Power $600M+)
- No named customers or production volume data

**Investment Status**: High risk - major catalyst (GM deal) did not occur, funding trajectory well below expectations.
