**Date**: September 4, 2025
**Location**: CHARLOTTE, N.C.

Honeywell (NASDAQ: HON) today announced that its portfolio company, Quantinuum, has raised approximately $600 million in equity capital in an oversubscribed round at a $10 billion pre-money equity valuation. This funding round includes participation from new investors Quanta Computer, NVentures (NVIDIA's venture capital arm), QED Investors, MESH, and Korea Investment Partners, in addition to returning investors JPMorganChase, Mitsui, Amgen, Cambridge Quantum Holdings, Serendipity Capital, and Honeywell. The capital will support Quantinuum's advancement of quantum computing commercialization, including the launch of Helios, the company's next-generation quantum system, the path toward achieving universal fault-tolerant computing, and continued development of full-stack quantum computing solutions.
