### Technical Coverage

**Helios Specifications** (per TQI article):
- **Physical Qubits**: 98 barium ions
- **Gate Fidelity**: 99.921% two-qubit gates, 99.9975% one-qubit gates
- **Logical Qubits**:
  - 48 error-corrected logical qubits (99.99% fidelity)
  - 94 error-detected logical qubits (globally entangled)
  - 50 error-detected logical qubits (magnetism simulation)
- **Architecture**: QCCD with first-of-its-kind commercial ion junction
- **Performance**: Better-than-physical (logical qubits outperform physical qubits)

**Technical Differentiation** (article analysis):
- Error correction breakthrough: First commercial system with 48 error-corrected logical qubits
- Fidelity leadership: 99.921% represents industry high-water mark
- Trapped-ion advantages: All-to-all connectivity, long coherence times
- Full-stack integration: Hardware + software + services
